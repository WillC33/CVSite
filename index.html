William Cooke

I am an aspiring web and software developer, with a passion for the process. Though I have always been interested in technology, my experiences have led me to realise this is the professional path that I am drawn to.

I have been running my own communication coaching practice for several years and though the work is satisfying, it was always the technical aspects of the operation that kept me feeling challenged and engaged.

Simply put, running a business requires one to become not only an expert in the service but also in the various technical support roles that go along with it! This means using software in a way that not everyone has the chance to.

I have created several WordPress sites for my projects, dealt with the integrations of various software products, fielded branding and design concepts, and consistently delivered my own work using Kanban and Agile project management.

That is where I discovered my true passion for development.

Furthermore, I am a skilled remote worker. I have lived and worked in 11 countries. I speak several languages to varying degrees of fluency and have highly developed communication skills. So, while I am still early on this path, I intend to push myself to learn and use my highly developed career and soft skills to help me.
